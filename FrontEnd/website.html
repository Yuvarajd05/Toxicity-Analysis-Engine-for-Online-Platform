CyberBullying Awareness

What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying involves online harassment through social media, gaming, or other platforms to scare, anger, or shame individuals.

How to Prevent Cyberbullying

  • Be kind and respectful online.
  • Think before you post.
  • Don't share sensitive details publicly.

The Rising Tide of Cyberbullying

With social media's rise, cyberbullying has become a growing concern for teens and young adults worldwide.
